Return & Refund Policy

We are committed to providing you with a satisfying shopping experience. If you need to return an item, we have implemented a clear and fair return policy to facilitate your experience.

Updated January 2025
1

Returns for Change of Mind or Size

You can return most items, with some exceptions

Not eligible: Opened sealed items (official brand seals or original packaging)
Not eligible: Mattresses, underwear, swimwear
Not eligible: TVs & Large Appliances
Not eligible: Large stapled items (sofas, furniture)
Return Conditions: You must return the item with its invoice and packaging in excellent condition.
Return Period: 10 business days from delivery date to initiate your return.
2

Returns for Defective Items

Defective, broken, non-functional, or counterfeit items

Returns are accepted for all types of items, except for categories not eligible for return.

Return Conditions: You must return the item with its invoice and packaging in excellent condition.
Return Deadline: 10 business days from delivery date.
Note: For categories not eligible for return, please contact our Customer Service.

Categories Not Eligible for Return

⚠️ Important
Mattresses
Printers & Software
Supermarkets & Groceries
Underwear & Swimwear
Beauty & Makeup
Perfumes & Wellness
Events & Tickets
"Second Chance" Items
Note: Second Chance products are not refundable for simple change of mind, damage to packaging, or minor cosmetic defects, if clearly stated in the product description.

Important Information

⚠️ We recommend that you carefully check your items upon receipt to avoid any disputes. Categories not eligible for return upon receipt. If you have any questions about a specific product's return eligibility, please contact our customer service team.
